Allocation Deep Dive — UHNI Portfolios

Ultra high net worth individuals — those with investable assets exceeding $30 million — have dramatically increased their large-cap equity exposure since 2021. Historically anchored in private equity, real estate, and alternative assets, the UHNI cohort is now one of the most significant structural buyers in public markets.

The driver: liquidity preference, inflation-hedging through quality compounders, and a generational shift toward tech-heavy portfolios. Family offices managing UHNI wealth report their largest single allocation shift in a decade — away from illiquid alternatives and into listed large-caps.
